## How It Works

**How does a transaction work on ElbowPay?**
ElbowPay defines the two users engaging on our platform as the Requester and the Matcher. The Requester and Matcher can equally post a request or match a request.
General Transaction Process:

Requester posts a request → Matcher matches the request posted → Both parties fulfill their requirements.

**What constitutes a successful transaction?**
- Requester must instruct Matcher to complete their part first
- Both Requester and Matcher must upload receipts of transfers
- Both Requester and Matcher must review and approve receipts before transaction expires

**Why must the Requester instruct Matcher to complete their part first?**
In order to ensure a safe, trustworthy platform, the requester is required to instruct the matcher to complete their part of the transaction first. This is because the Requester has a security deposit held on the platform which serves as a guarantee of commitment. This process helps build trust, reduce fraud and disputes and ensuring accountability and protecting both users.

**How can I post as a Requester?**
- Post: Post a request about your money needs super quick and easy using the + icon on the Explore Screen
- Match: A matcher finds your posts and sends you a match request to accept
- Wallet: Requesters are required to deposit an amount equal to the transaction request amount into their ElbowPay wallet. The funds are held in escrow until all transaction requirements are fulfilled
- Chat: Use the chat icon in transactions tab to communicate with your match. Negotiate rates and come to a mutual agreement
- Exchange: Exchange money using your preferred methods (Zelle, bank transfer, mobile money, etc.)
  - Person A sends USD to Person B in the US
  - Person B sends money to Person A's recipient abroad

- Confirm: Requester confirms receipt of payment from Matcher. Requester submits proof of payment
Settlement: Security deposit is refunded to Requester

**How can I match a request?**
- Search: Find a request you are capable of fulfilling on the explore page
- Match: Match the request using the match button. When you match a request, it is automatically an accepted request.
- Wallet: Matchers are charged fees for use of the platform to fulfill a request.
- Chat: Use the chat icon in transactions tab to communicate with your match. Negotiate rates and come to a mutual agreement
- Exchange: Exchange money using preferred methods (Zelle, bank transfer, mobile money, etc.)
    - Person A sends USD to Person B in the US
    - Person B sends money to Person A's recipient abroad

- Confirm: Matcher submits proof of payment. Matcher confirms receipt of payment from Requester

**How long does a transaction take?**
Length of transactions depends on both parties' response time and method used.

## Wallet & Security Deposits

**What is the wallet deposit and why do I need one?**
The wallet deposit is a temporary hold on funds that ensures both parties complete their side of the transaction. Think of it as escrow—it discourages cancellations and no-shows. Once your exchange partner confirms receipt, the deposit is released back to you in full.

**Why do you require a deposit?**
The wallet deposit creates accountability. It ensures senders actually send and receivers actually pay out. Without it, either party could back out mid-transaction with no consequence. Your deposit is fully refunded the moment the exchange completes successfully.

**Who funds the wallet?**
Usually the person initiating the transaction.

**Is the security deposit refunded in full?**
Yes, the security deposit made by the requester when they fund their wallet is refunded upon fulfillment of all transaction exchange requirements minus ElbowPay's fees. Completion of transaction requirements is mandatory.

**What happens to my security deposit if a transaction fails?**
If the transaction fails due to the matcher's non-performance or system-verified issues, the requester's security deposit is fully refunded.
If a user violates platform rules or cancels after the matcher has completed their obligation, the requester's security deposit may be partially or fully forfeited.

## Refunds

**Are ElbowPay fees refundable?**
ElbowPay fees are refundable when:

- A posted request is not matched before it expires
- When a funded request is deleted or cancelled before it is matched

**Are transaction refunds available on ElbowPay?**
Refunds on ElbowPay depend on the status of the transaction. Since ElbowPay is a peer-to-peer exchange platform, funds are only refundable if the transaction has been successfully completed or transaction expires.

**When can I receive a refund?**
You may be eligible for a refund if:

- The matcher fails to complete their part of the transaction within the agreed timeframe
- The request is cancelled before any funds are released
- A dispute is raised and resolved in your favor
- The posted request expires

**Are completed transaction deposits refundable?**
Yes.

**How long does a refund take to process?**
Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment method and financial institution.

**How do I request a refund or raise a dispute?**
You can request a refund or raise a dispute directly within the ElbowPay app by navigating to the transaction details and selecting "Refund". Our support team will review and resolve the case promptly.

**Does ElbowPay charge a refund fee?**
ElbowPay does not charge a refund fee; however, third-party payment providers may apply processing fees, which are outside of ElbowPay's control.
